Natural Filtration for Greywater

A soak pit is a simple, effective method for greywater disposal. It filters water naturally by letting it seep into the soil. Ideal for handwashing stations, kitchen waste, and low-volume greywater reuse.

Soak Pit Animation

Construction Tips

  • Dig a pit ~1m wide × 1m deep, filled with stones or gravel
  • Line the bottom with sand for slow filtration
  • Use a perforated drum or basket to prevent clogging
  • Cover with a slab or secure mesh for safety
