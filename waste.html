Fuel Briquette Production

Fuel Briquettes from Agricultural, Paper Waste, Charcoal Dust, Sawdust and Ash Binding Ingredients: Common binders include cassava flour, clay, molasses, paper pulp, and wood ash. The ideal ratio is typically 5-10% binder to 90-95% biomass.

Fuel Briquette Stove

Efficient Stove Design: Rocket stoves work exceptionally well with biomass briquettes, featuring an L-shaped combustion chamber that creates efficient airflow. The insulated vertical chimney creates a strong draft, ensuring complete combustion and minimal smoke. Adding a metal grate at the bottom improves airflow and allows ash to fall away, while a small feeding shelf makes adding briquettes convenient without disturbing the cooking pot.
