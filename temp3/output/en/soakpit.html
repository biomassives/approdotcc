Natural filtration for greywater disposal—simple, effective, and low-cost.

A soak pit filters greywater naturally by letting it seep into the soil. Ideal for handwashing stations, kitchen waste, and low-volume greywater reuse.

Soak Pit Animation

Construction Tips

  • Dig a pit about 1 m wide × 1 m deep, then fill with stones or gravel.
  • Line the bottom with sand to slow down filtration and prevent wash-through.
  • Insert a perforated drum or plastic basket to stop solids from clogging the pit.
  • Cover with a concrete slab or secure metal mesh for child and animal safety.
